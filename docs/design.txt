h4. entity relationship
# Party, PartyGroup and UserLogin
	PartyGroup的creator: Party中保存了createdByUserLogin
	从UserLogin中获得对应的Party/Person
		UserLogin --partyId-- Party --partyId-- Person
		UserLogin --partyId-- Person
	Party/Person所属的公司
		Employment
			PartyIdFrom 作为 INTERNAL_ORGANIZATIO 角色(RoleTypeIdFrom) 
			partyIdTo 作为 EMPLOYEE (RoleTypeIdTo) 角色
			于 FromDate 建立雇佣关系
		PartyRelationship
			party relationship type:	
				child, parent
				account, parent account
				assistant, employee, lead owner(lead owned by), lead owners/managers, lead relation, manager, owner(owned by), reports to, sales_affiliate
				agent, distributor, group rollup(group member)
				contact relation, 
				customer, partner, spouse, supplier
				friend, 
				host server visitor, isp visitor, web master
			partyIdFrom 作为 INTERNAL_ORGANIZATIO 角色(RoleTypeIdFrom)
			partyIdTo 作为 EMPLOYEE (RoleTypeIdTo) 角色
			于 FromDate 建立 EMPLOYMENT (PartyRelationshipTypeId) 关系
# Party/PartyGroup vs. Content
	Party --PartyContent-- Content
                |
            PartyContentType
	PartyContentType:
		internal content, logo image url, user defined content, vendor shipping info
